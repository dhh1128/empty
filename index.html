A
Alcubierre drive #hardscience#scifi

A speculative warp drive idea based on a solution of Einstein's field equations in general relativity as proposed by theoretical physicist Miguel Alcubierre during his PhD study at the University of Wales, Cardiff, by which a spacecraft could achieve apparent faster-than-light travel if a configurable energy-density field lower than that of the vacuum (that is, negative mass) could be created. [from Wikipedia, CC-BY-SA 3.0]

faster than light #scifi

A general description of any technology that allows movement between point A and point B in less time than light could traverse the distance. This includes propulsion systems that warp space-time, tesseracts, the infinite improbability drive, wormholes, and so forth.

Synonym for faster than light.

K
Krypton #dccomics#scifi

The home planet of Kal-el (the secret identity of Superman, the DC Comics superhero).

Kal-el is "sole survivor of a cataclysm on the planet Krypton, sent in a space capsule to Earth, where he is raised from infancy (effectively as an illegal immigrant) by US foster-parents, the character's dual identity as timid reporter Clark Kent and indestructible, patriotic crime-fighter Superman has a basic appeal to readers." [from The Encyclopedia of Science Fiction, fair use]

P
parsec #hardscience#hgttg#scifi#startrek#starwars

The parsec (symbol: pc) is a unit of length used to measure the large distances to astronomical objects outside the Solar System, approximately equal to 3.26 light-years. [from Wikipedia, CC-BY-SA 3.0]

tesseract #scifi

A shortcut that achieves faster-than-light travel by taking advantage of a fifth dimension.

W
warp #scifi#startrek

A measure of how much space-time is distorted by a field. Warp is the basis of faster-than-light technologies such as the Alcubierre drive.
